About Me

Storytelling With Purpose

I'm Grace Akpezi Edwin-Okon — an award-winning Nigerian filmmaker, director, producer, screenwriter, author, and creative entrepreneur whose career spans over two decades across film, television, animation, talent development, and creative industry leadership.

My journey began in 1999 when I won the Miss Face of DELSU Pageant and later became Second Runner-Up at the Most Beautiful Girl in Nigeria (MBGN) Pageant. A graduate of English Language from Delta State University, I transitioned from modelling and acting into writing, directing, and producing, discovering my passion for creating meaningful stories that entertain, educate, and inspire social change.

Today, as Founder of Derwin Film Academy and National Vice President of the Directors Guild of Nigeria, I am dedicated to using film as a powerful tool for education, advocacy, empowerment, and social transformation.
